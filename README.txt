# ISA401 Final Project: Affordability Comparison 2010 v. 2022

## Author
Cam Townswick, Annie Freeman, & Sydney Booth

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Census, BLS, and CPI data to compare the relative affordability and economic landscape for people ages (~22-34) from 2010 vs. 2022. The goal is to determine how affordability has changed over time for newer generations in the workforce.
