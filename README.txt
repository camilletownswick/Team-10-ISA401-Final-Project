# ISA401 Final Project: Affordability Comparison 2010 v. 2022

## Author
Cam Townswick, Annie Freeman, & Sydney Booth

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Census, BLS, and CPI data to compare the relative affordability and economic landscape for people ages (~22-44) from 2010 vs. 2022. The goal is to determine how affordability has changed over time for newer generations in the workforce.

This matters because income alone does not tell the full story. Even if wages or household income have increased since 2010, housing costs, rent, inflation, and living expenses may have increased faster. This project will compare income, housing costs, general price levels, and affordability indicators across time to evaluate whether Americans ages 25–44 are financially better or worse off.
